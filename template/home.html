We are working on a small project for the betterment of our university life on how mask and other essentials can be distributed seamlessly allover the university when it will open after covid pandemic.
Hope you all will cooperate with us. A screenshot of the submission form is shown at the right side. Instructions for the form submission are given below:

* You can skip this instruction and see the 4-min video link given in navigation bar.(Recommended *)
* For any queries you can reach us at: contactus.crowdsourcing@gmail.com
1. You have to choose 3 locations inside our university where you think it will be best to put the distribution centres.
2. Click on "Click here to view the map" button and see the map. Coordinates are given as decimal numbers along X and Y direction for sake of simplicity. You can zoom in and zoom out the map.
3. Place the first coordinate values in the place "Coodinate1". eg- ( X:100 Y:150 ). Input in one or two sentences that why you have choosen the corresponding coordinate. (eg: Coordinate1 is near PG hostel.).
4. Repeat same process for coordinate2 and coordinate3. Please make sure that distance between any two coordinates are >=10. (*Note: Distance is auto calculated and has been shown in the form.)
5. Put an rating weightage preference for each location such that total of 3 rating values for 3 coordinates is =10. eg: Lets say I prefer location 2 much than other two location. So, I shall put rating for coordinate1: 3. rating for coordinate2: 4, rating for coordinate3: 3. Total=3+4+3=10.
*Note: Sum will be auto calculated and shown.
6. Repeat same process for coordinate2 and coordinate3.
